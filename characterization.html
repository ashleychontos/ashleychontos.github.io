Exoplanet Detection & Characterization

[A Very Brief Synopsis]

The NASA Kepler space telescope demonstrated that planets are ubiquitous, with more planets than stars, completely revolutionizing the understanding of our place in the Universe. With an estimated 100 billion stars in the Milky Way Galaxy alone and likely even more galaxies, the seemingly infinite number of planets suggests that we are probably not alone in the Universe. However, finding another alien civilization is not an easy quest and therefore remains one of the primary science goals of the NASA administration.
